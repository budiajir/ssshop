📖 Our Story

Hi! Welcome to Ssshophaus Boulder. Let us introduce ourselves before your visit.

We are located at Setrasari Plaza, Bandung, Indonesia. The space is on the third floor of an open-plan shophouse unit.

This place started as my personal training space to build endurance, so I decided to build a training wall. The wall consists of only four plywood panels, making it 2.4 meters high and 4.8 meters wide, with various types of handholds and small footholds.

Long story short, I decided to create an Instagram account for this space. The intention was simply to let anyone come train and hang out. Over time, this place has become a little melting pot — from old friends I hadn't seen in a while, to friends of friends, and even complete strangers.

Hopefully this short introduction gives you a better picture of the space. See you soon!
